Solar System

The Sun contains 99.85% of all matter in the Solar System. the planets, which condensed out of the same disk of material that formed Sun, contain only 0.135% of the mass of the solar system. Jupiter contains more than twice the matter of all the other planets combined. Satellites of the planets, comets, asteroids, meteoroids, and the interplanar medium constitute the remaining 0.015%. the following table is a list of the mass distribution within our Solar System.

Variabel Value
Sun 99.85%
Planets 0.135%
Comets 0.01%
Satellites 0.00005%
Minor planets 0.0000002%
Interplanetary 0.0000001%
